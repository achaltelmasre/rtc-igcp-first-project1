Specialitity of Aurangabad


Aurangabad, the Tourism Capital of Maharashtra, was named after the Mughal emperor Aurangzeb. The city is a beautiful blend of history, culture, and nature. From massive forts to perfectly manicured lawns to historic sites, the city has an array of places to visit. Those planning a trip to Aurangabad, we are certain that you need a long vacation to explore the city.
