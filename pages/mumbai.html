Specialitity of Mumbai

Mumbai lies on the Konkan coast on the west coast of India and has a deep natural harbour In 2008, Mumbai was named an alpha world city. Mumbai. Bombay.

The best time to visit Mumbai is from October to February when the city has its winter. This is the perfect time for traveling and sightseeing as the weather is moderate and you can comfortably walk about the city as you explore its glory and mysteries.
